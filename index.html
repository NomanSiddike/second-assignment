BMI Calculator

A BMI calculator estimates body fat using height and weight, helping determine if you're underweight, normal, overweight, or obese. It’s a simple tool, but not fully accurate for everyone.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 - and Above Obese

Calculate your BMI

To calculate your BMI, divide your weight (kg) by your height (m) squared. It assesses body fat levels.
